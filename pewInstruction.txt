You are flying into a star field

The goal of this game is to shoot enemies that fly around you

Use the arrow keys to move the ship
Press space to shoot a missile. All the missiles travel towards the center of
the starfield.

You have a limited number of bullets. However, green ammo packs will spawn in
the field. Collect them to gain more ammo.
Red health packs will also spawn in the field. Collect them to recover health.

Be careful. Touching the enemy's bullets will hurt you

Press q to quit the game and r to restart the game

Press the back button to go back
